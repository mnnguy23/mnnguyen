Latest Projects

Guestographer

A crowd-sourcing photo service that transforms your guests into a coordinated photography team.

New

By 2017, it is predicted that more than 78% of all photos taken will be from a smartphone. Quality camera phones and a wide range of photo editing apps have given every single one of us the ability to become a great photographer. Ironically, event directors spend a significant amount of money and time hiring professional photographers to take similar pictures and compiling these images. guestographer seeks to bridge the gap between attendees who take photos and event directors who are seeking to obtain them in an engaging and efficient experience.

With our service, Hosts are given a guestographer phone number to distribute to all of their guests. The guests can text their photos to this number the pictures are automatically complied and consolidated into an album for all to view throughout the event.
